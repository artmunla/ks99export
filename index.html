About Us
Farm-to-Port Supply from Sisaket

KS 99 EXPORT, a division of KS.99 Engineering & Service Co., Ltd., specializes in the export of premium Thai shallots sourced directly from Sisaket — Thailand's leading shallot-producing region.

We operate a fully integrated farm-to-port supply chain, working closely with local growers to ensure consistent quality, proper drying, and reliable grading. Each batch is carefully sorted, packed according to buyer specifications, and prepared with complete export documentation for international shipment.

Our commitment is to deliver stable quality, transparent communication, and dependable logistics, enabling our partners to source Thai shallots with confidence and efficiency.

How We Work
8-Step Export Workflow

From buyer inquiry to shipment release — clear process, faster communication, and practical execution

Step 01
📩

Receive Inquiry

Buyer shares grade, quantity, packing, destination, and preferred Incoterm.

Step 02
💰

Send Quotation

We issue quotation with product details, trade terms, payment terms, and validity period.

Step 03
📋

Order Confirmation

Buyer confirms PO or accepts PI so procurement and shipment planning can begin.

Step 04
🌿

Source Product

Procurement is arranged from Sisaket supply sources according to agreed quality and volume.

Step 05
🔬

Grade & Pack

Shallots are sorted, packed, and prepared for delivery to port or container loading point.

Step 06
🚢

Book Shipment

Transport and shipping schedule are coordinated according to buyer destination and cargo readiness.

Step 07
📄

Prepare Documents

Commercial documents and required export certificates are arranged for customs and buyer clearance.

Step 08
✅

Release & Follow Up

Shipment details, copies of documents, and logistics updates are shared with the buyer.
